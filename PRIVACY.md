# Privacy Policy for Glide

**Last Updated:** December 29, 2025

## Overview

Glide ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Glide handles your information when you use our Chrome extension for tracking and navigating AI chat conversations.

## Information Collection and Use

### What We Collect
Glide collects and stores the following information **locally on your device only**:
- Text content of your messages in AI chat conversations
- Timestamps of when messages were captured
- Conversation URLs and titles
- Your current scroll position within conversations

### How We Store Data
- All data is stored **locally** using Chrome's built-in storage API
- Data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers
- We do not have access to your data
- We do not operate any servers to collect or process your information

### What We DON'T Collect
- Personal identifying information
- Usernames, passwords, or authentication tokens
- Browsing history outside of supported AI chat sites
- Any analytics or usage statistics
- Cookies or tracking identifiers

## Data Transmission

**Glide does not transmit any data externally.** Your conversation history never leaves your computer. The extension operates entirely within your browser without any external connections to our servers or third-party services.

## Third-Party Services

Glide does not integrate with, share data with, or transmit data to any third-party services, including:
- Analytics platforms
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage services
- External databases

## Permissions Explained

Glide requires the following Chrome permissions to function:

### Storage
- **Purpose:** To save your chat messages locally on your device
- **Scope:** Only stores data you explicitly interact with on supported sites

### Tabs
- **Purpose:** To detect when you visit supported AI chat platforms
- **Scope:** Only monitors URLs to identify Claude.ai, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot

### Active Tab
- **Purpose:** To read and track messages from the current chat conversation
- **Scope:** Only reads content from active tabs on supported platforms

### Scripting
- **Purpose:** To inject functionality for tracking messages and navigation
- **Scope:** Only runs on supported AI chat websites

### Host Permissions
- **Supported Sites:**
  - https://claude.ai/*
  - https://chatgpt.com/*
  - https://gemini.google.com/*
  - https://copilot.microsoft.com/*
- **Purpose:** To enable message tracking on these specific platforms only

## Data Security

- All data is stored using Chrome's secure storage API
- Data is sandboxed within your browser profile
- Only you have access to your stored conversations
- Data is automatically cleared if you uninstall the extension

## Data Retention

- Conversations are retained locally until you manually clear them or uninstall the extension
- You can clear stored data at any time by removing the extension
- Chrome's storage API automatically manages storage limits

## Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:
- **Access:** All data is stored locally and accessible only to you
- **Delete:** Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data
- **Export:** You can access your data through Chrome's extension storage
- **Control:** You can disable the extension at any time without losing Chrome functionality

## Children's Privacy

Glide does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is designed for general use and does not target children specifically.
